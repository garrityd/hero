My Hero

Heros are very important to people. A hero helps give confidence andalso can help guide where someone takes their life. A hero can allow for self improvement and give the courage and will to keep going even when times are tough. This hero site is dedicated to my hero, Albert Einstein.

Albert Einstein is my hero because he had the courage to go against an ancient system of mathematics that did not fully describe how the universe worked. He broke new ground in physiucs and mathematics which allowed for much of the technology thay we have today. Einstein created the calculations that allow us to study the stars and black holes. We are still today working on new ways to use his calculations such as fusion reactors which are a safe alternative power source that has no carbon or waste output.

Other Hero Options

Isaac Newton

Isaac Newton created the fundamental equations used in physics and played an important role in the advancement of the science of physics. His equations were used for hundreds of years are still are used for simple physics.

Nikola Tesla

Nikola Tesla pioneered the generation, transmission, and the used of alternating current in machines. These advancements allowed for further technology of electricity to be developed.

Galileo Galilei

He questioned the idea behind an Earth-Centric model of the solar system and the universe. This allowed for a breakthrough in gravity and the understanding of the planets and the stars.

Marie Curie

Marie Curie discovered the effects of radiation and two seperate elements: radium and polonium. She was also the first person to ever win two seperate nobel prizes.
